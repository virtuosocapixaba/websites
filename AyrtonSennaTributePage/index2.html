Ayrton Senna

The greatest Formula 1 driver of all time

Ayrton Senna da Silva

Ayrton Senna is regarded by many as the greatest Formula 1 driver of all time.
His quick racing pace, amazing qualifying laps, incredible car control in the wet, and daring overtakes made him a legend of motorsport.
His unique driving style really impressed fans and other racing drivers.
His sudden death at the San Marino GP in 1994 shocked the world and ended a brilliant racing career.

Although his career ended suddenly Senna managed to achieve some very impressive feats in Formula One:

3 world titles
41 race wins
65 pole positions
80 podium finishes
19 fastest laps

Senna became an inspiration to many successful Formula One drivers such as Fernando Alonso and Lewis Hamilton.
